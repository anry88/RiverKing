0) Общий “Series Spec” (вставляй в каждый промпт без изменений)

OUTPUT: PNG 1024×1024, transparent background.

STYLE (fixed for the whole series):
- clean vector shapes, vintage flat badge, minimal details
- solid fills only (NO gradients, NO glow, NO vignette, NO textures, NO noise, NO text)
- 1–2 large flat shadow shapes (as separate solid shapes), consistent direction: shadow goes 12px down and 8px right
- consistent dark outline everywhere: stroke thickness 12px, rounded joins/caps

GEOMETRY (fixed):
- Badge center: (512, 512)
- Outer circle radius: 472px (outer diameter 944px). Everything must fit inside this circle; outside is fully transparent.
- Outer ring thickness: 118px (so inner radius of outer ring = 354px)
- Inner light rim (thin ring) thickness: 22px (so inner radius after rim = 332px)
- Inner field (main circle background) radius: 332px
- Central achievement symbol must fit inside a 560×560px box centered at (512,512). Keep at least 36px padding from the inner light rim.
- Edge constraint: all 4 corners (0,0), (1023,0), (0,1023), (1023,1023) must be fully transparent.
- Outside the outer radius (472px) is transparent only.

OUTER RING (two-halves split, like the reference):
- The outer ring fill is made of EXACTLY 2 solid shapes (no third shading shape):
  1) TOP semi-annulus (upper half) — uniform flat fill, lighter tone
  2) BOTTOM semi-annulus (lower half) — uniform flat fill, darker tone
- The split is a perfectly straight horizontal line through the center (y = 512).
- The split line has NO stroke/outline of its own (only the ring’s normal outlines).
- No gradients, no patches, no mottling, no texture, no extra highlights/shadows on the ring.
- Only anti-aliasing on the curved edges is allowed.
- Use ONLY two colors on the ring fill: OuterRingTop and OuterRingBottom.

LAYER ORDER (fixed):
1) Outer ring
2) Thin inner light rim
3) Inner field circle
4) Ornaments for tier (rivets / star / laurel / crown) — only where specified
5) Central symbol (the only thing that changes per badge)
All elements use the same outline rules above.

NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS:
no typography, no labels, no numbers, no watermark, no photo/3D render, no metallic reflections, no sparkles/glitter, no gradients.

Как вставлять символ достижения (важно для консистентности)

Вместо [КРАТКО ОПИШИ СИМВОЛ ДОСТИЖЕНИЯ] используй формулировку такого типа:

a simple flat icon of [X], centered, left-to-right balanced, 2–5 solid shapes, no micro-details, no texture, same 12px outline, fits 560×560 box


Примеры:

a simple flat icon of a fish hanging vertically above water (no hook), minimal fins, centered

a simple flat icon of a trophy cup, centered, minimal details
